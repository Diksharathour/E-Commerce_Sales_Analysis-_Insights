## **E-Commerce_Sales_Analysis_And_Insights**


## **Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior, membership performance, customer satisfaction, and revenue trends in an e-commerce business using SQL and Power BI. The `E_Commerce_Sales` dataset includes detailed information such as Customer_id,	Gender,	Age, City,	Membership,	Total_Spend, Items_Purchased, Average_Rating, Discount,	Last_Purchase, Satisfaction.
The objective was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that can help an e-commerce business understand its customers, improve customer retention, and identify revenue growth opportunities. Using SQL for data analysis and Power BI for visualization, I explored customer demographics, spending patterns, membership performance, discount effectiveness, and customer segmentation.

## **Objectives**

1. **Database Setup-** Import and organize the Bank Customer Churn dataset into a structured SQL database to enable efficient data storage, querying, and analysis.
2. **Data Cleaning and Preparation-** Perform data quality checks by identifying and handling duplicate, missing, and inconsistent records to ensure accurate and reliable analysis.
3. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)-** Analyze customer demographics, account characteristics, and geographic distributions to identify patterns, trends, and factors associated with customer churn.
4. **Customer Analysis Using SQL-** Utilize SQL queries to answer key business questions, including:
- Churn trends by geography, gender, and age group.
- Impact of account activity, product usage, and balance on churn behavior.
- Identification of high-risk customer segments.
5. **Customer Segmentation and Risk Assessment-** Develop a churn-risk segmentation model by categorizing customers based on behavioral and demographic attributes to support targeted retention strategies.
6. **Interactive Dashboard Development-** Build an interactive Power BI dashboard to monitor key churn metrics, customer segments, retention indicators, and business performance through dynamic visualizations and filters.
7. **Insights and Recommendations-** Generate actionable insights to help stakeholders understand churn drivers, improve customer retention, enhance customer lifetime value, and support data-driven decision-making.

## **Business Problem**

E-commerce businesses generate large amounts of customer data every day. However, identifying valuable customers, understanding purchasing behavior, and recognizing customers at risk of leaving can be challenging. This project aims to answer key business questions such as:

Q.1 Which membership plan generates the highest revenue?

Q.2 Which cities contribute the most sales?

Q.3 Which customer age groups spend the most?

Q.4 Do discounts influence customer spending behavior?

Q.5 Which customer segments are most valuable?

Q.6 Which customers are at risk of becoming inactive?

## Analysis Performed

### Revenue Analysis

* Evaluated revenue contribution by membership type.
* Identified top-performing cities based on total sales.
* Analyzed spending behavior across different age groups.

### Customer Behavior Analysis

* Compared spending patterns of customers using discounts versus those who do not.
* Evaluated customer satisfaction across membership categories.
* Measured average spending and purchase volume.

## Key Business Insights

- Membership programs play a significant role in overall revenue generation.
- Customer spending behavior varies across age groups.
- Customer segmentation helps identify high-value customers for targeted marketing.
- Monitoring inactive customers can help reduce customer churn.
- Discount strategies can influence customer purchasing behavior.
- Customer satisfaction trends can provide valuable guidance for improving retention.

## **Business Recommendations**

1. **Strengthen High-Value Customer Retention**
- Offer personalized discounts and early access to new products.
- Create targeted retention campaigns to reduce customer attrition.

2. **Optimize Membership Programs**
- Promote membership upgrades through exclusive benefits and personalized offers.
- Analyze customer journeys to identify opportunities for membership conversion.

3. **Focus Marketing Efforts on High-Performing Cities**
- Increase marketing investments in top-performing cities to maximize revenue growth.
- Conduct localized campaigns in lower-performing cities to improve customer acquisition.

4. **Encourage Higher Spending Among Medium-Value Customers**
- Implement cross-selling and upselling strategies.
- Recommend complementary products based on previous purchases.

5. **Monitor Customer Purchase Behavior**
- Track customer buying frequency and spending trends.
- Launch re-engagement campaigns for customers showing reduced activity.
